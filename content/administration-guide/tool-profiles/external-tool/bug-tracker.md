TestArchitect can be integrated with issue tracking systems, enabling you and your team to easily monitor and update the status of bug issues by associating them with TestArchitect bugs.

Currently, TestArchitect supports connections with the following issue tracking tool\(s\):

-   [JIRA](/user-guide/integration-with-third-party-tools/jira-integration/configuring-jira-integration/registering-a-jira-server)
-   [Team Foundation Server](/user-guide/integration-with-third-party-tools/tfs-integration/tfs-bug-tracker/configuring-tfs-bug-tracker-integration/registering-a-tfs-bug-tracker#)
